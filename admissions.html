How to apply

Three clear steps to get started.

The school does not replace the WCED application process. Parents and guardians should complete the official online application and keep their supporting documents ready.

01

Check the dates

Admission windows and placement timelines are published by the Western Cape Education Department.

View current WCED information ↗
02

Prepare documents

Certified identification, proof of residence, the learner’s latest report and primary-school health records may be required.

See the checklist ↓
03

Apply and follow up

Submit the application through WCED, record the reference details and respond promptly to placement communication.

Contact pupil administration →

Document checklist

Have the essentials ready.

WCED requirements can vary by application type. The official admissions page should always be treated as the final source of information.

Check official requirements ↗
✓

Identification

Learner and parent or caregiver identification, birth certificate or applicable immigration documents.

✓

Proof of residence

A rates account, lease agreement or qualifying affidavit confirming the home address.

✓

Latest school report

The learner’s most recent official school or academic report, where applicable.

✓

Health record

An immunisation card or Road to Health chart for primary-school applications.
