Welcome to The Ridge

Rooted in community.
Ready for tomorrow.

We nurture confident, curious learners through excellent teaching, meaningful relationships and opportunities beyond the classroom.

Our co-educational, English-medium school combines academic focus with technology, coding, robotics, sport, music, art and service. Every learner is encouraged to discover their strengths and contribute with respect.

Who we are

A proud history with a clear purpose.

Blouberg Ridge has grown with its community while keeping the values, identity and sense of belonging that make The Ridge distinctive.

Our story

Named for the ridge. Built for the community.

The school takes its name from the ridge that runs parallel to the Blouberg coastline. From its campus in Wessels Road, Bloubergrant, The Ridge has become a thriving co-educational, English-medium primary school.

1990Opened in January for Grades 1–3.
1991Expanded to include Grades 4–7.
TodayA growing school community focused on excellent, holistic learning.
“

Our mission

The Ridge is a safe, values-driven and nurturing environment where we embrace diversity, innovation and holistic learning.

Our badge

Labore Vinces

The badge features Table Mountain and the sea—the world-famous view across the bay from Blouberg. Its motto means “By effort, we succeed.” Navy, light blue and yellow form the school colours.

Our school song carries the same spirit of learning, teamwork, pride and service.

Our campus

At home in Bloubergrant

The Wessels Road campus combines state-owned facilities with additional buildings supported by the Blouberg Ridge Primary Trust, giving learners space to learn, create, play and grow.
