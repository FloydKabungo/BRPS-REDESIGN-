Our approach

Excellent teaching with the learner at the centre.

Learning at The Ridge is structured, engaging and supportive.

Teachers use technology, research and purposeful classroom practice to help learners understand deeply, think independently and apply what they know. Progress matters, but so do confidence, character and a genuine love of learning.

01

Strong foundations

Literacy, numeracy and confident communication are developed carefully from the earliest grades.

02

Active learning

Discussion, practical tasks, technology and problem-solving turn lessons into meaningful experiences.

03

Care and challenge

High expectations are balanced with a safe, values-driven environment and support when it is needed.

Grades 1 to 7

A learning journey designed to grow with every child.

Each phase has its own rhythm and priorities, while building steadily towards confident, independent learning.

Building the basics

A confident start to school life.

The Foundation Phase focuses on the skills and habits that make all future learning possible. Learners grow through language-rich classrooms, practical mathematics, creative activity, movement and structured routines.

  • Home Language and First Additional Language
  • Mathematics and early problem-solving
  • Life Skills, creativity and physical development
  • Growing independence and positive classroom habits
ABC

Read.Count.Create.

Expanding knowledge

More subjects. Deeper thinking.

In the Intermediate Phase, learners move into more specialised subject learning. They are encouraged to ask questions, organise their work, communicate ideas and connect knowledge across different areas.

  • Languages and Mathematics
  • Natural Sciences and Technology
  • Social Sciences and Life Skills
  • Research, collaboration and independent study habits

Question.Explore.Connect.

Ready for what comes next

Independence, leadership and preparation.

Grade 7 brings together academic knowledge, personal responsibility and leadership. Learners strengthen subject understanding and the organisation, resilience and confidence needed for high school.

  • Broader subject demands and formal assessment
  • Economic and Management Sciences
  • Leadership, accountability and teamwork
  • Preparation for the transition to high school

Lead.Achieve.Advance.

Learning support

Every learner should feel known and supported.

Learning needs do not look the same for every child. The Ridge has a dedicated learning-support team that works with educators and families to identify needs, strengthen skills and help learners participate more confidently.
